AMRITA VISHWA VIDYAPEETHAM

About Amrita University
 Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, also known as Amrita University,
 is a multi-campus private deemed university in India, headquartered in Ettimadai, Coimbatore. 
 It has 19 constituent schools spread across ten campuses in various locations, including Coimbatore,
 Amritapuri (Kollam),
 Kochi, Bengaluru, Amaravati, Chennai, Faridabad, Mysore, Nagercoil, and Haridwar.
