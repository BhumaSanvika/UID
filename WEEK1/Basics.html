about amrita

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-disciplinary, research-intensive private university with a student strength of over 30000,It was founded in 2003 and is inspired by the vision of Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi. The university has campuses across India and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, medicine, arts, science, and management.
